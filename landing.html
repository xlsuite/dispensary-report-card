How it works

Three steps to your report card

1

Paste your URL

We only look at public pages, nothing to install. Just drop in your homepage URL and hit scan.

2

We run 30+ checks

Platform tier, sitemaps, product pages, pixels, email capture, loyalty program, Google Business Profile — all scanned automatically.

3

Get your grade + fix list

Every failing check comes with plain-English instructions. The report opens with the five fixes that would raise your score most.

What we check

Seven categories, 30+ signals

Local Search & GBP

Where dispensary customers actually find you. Rating, reviews, photos, per-store deep links.

SEO Fundamentals

Titles, descriptions, structured data, sitemaps. The basics that most dispensaries still miss.

E-commerce & Platform

The big one: can Google even see your menu? Iframe menus are invisible to search.

Email Marketing

Capture forms and whether your ESP can do abandoned-cart revenue recovery.

Loyalty Program

The retention engine. We detect AlpineIQ, Springbig, Spark Rewards and more.

Retention & Reviews

Post-purchase flows and review platforms. Are you collecting social proof?

Analytics & Tracking

GA4, Tag Manager, Search Console. Ad pixels reported as info — we don't penalize for policy.
